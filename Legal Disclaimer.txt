2. PROBABILITY AND EXPECTATIONS
=============================================================================

USERS MUST UNDERSTAND:

The probability of randomly generating a Bitcoin wallet with funds is:
- Approximately 1 in 2^256 (for private keys)
- Approximately 1 in 2^160 (for addresses)

To put this in perspective:
- There are more possible Bitcoin addresses than atoms in the observable universe
- Random generation will NOT find funded wallets
- This is a DEMONSTRATION of cryptographic security strength

Expected Results:
- Success rate for random generation: ~0.00000000000000000000000000000001%
- This tool will likely NEVER find a funded wallet through random generation
- Any success would be statistically equivalent to impossible
